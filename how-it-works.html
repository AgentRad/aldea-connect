No waitlist · 48-hour matches

From worry to first appointment — in days.

Most families on Aldea Connect go from intake to a confirmed visit in under a week. Here’s exactly how that works.

  1. 1

    Tell us about your child

    A 3-minute intake covers age, concerns, location, language preference and insurance. No diagnosis required to start.

  2. 2

    Get matched in 48 hours

    A care navigator personally reviews your intake and hand-picks 2–3 providers with real openings. We verify benefits before you book.

  3. 3

    Book and begin care

    Schedule directly from your inbox, meet your provider, and start the right plan for your child — usually within a week.

What makes us different

  • No waitlist. We only list providers with active openings.
  • Hand-matched. A real human reviews your intake — not a search algorithm.
  • Free for families. Providers pay a flat subscription so we never take a cut of your care.
  • Insurance verified upfront. No surprise bills after your first visit.
  • One care plan. If your child needs evaluation, ABA and speech, we coordinate it under one roof.

A typical timeline

  1. Day 0 — submit intake
  2. Day 1 — care navigator calls or emails
  3. Day 2 — 2–3 hand-picked matches in your inbox
  4. Day 3–5 — book directly, benefits verified
  5. Day 5–10 — first appointment
