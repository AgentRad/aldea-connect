Wondering if it’s time for an evaluation?

This short screener is parent-reported and not a diagnosis. It can help you decide whether a clinical evaluation is worth scheduling — and we can match you with one if it is.

Autism & ADHD parent screener

1. My child struggles to make and keep friends their own age.
2. My child has very specific interests they focus on intensely.
3. Changes in routine cause significant distress.
4. My child has trouble staying focused on a task for their age.
5. Teachers or caregivers have raised concerns about my child’s development.
6. My child has strong reactions to sensory experiences (sounds, textures, lights).
