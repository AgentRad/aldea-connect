Privacy

Privacy policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026 · This is a starter template, not legal advice.

Aldea Connect (“we”, “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of the families and providers who use our platform. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Intake information: the details you share when requesting a match — child age, concerns, location, language and insurance.
  • Account information: name, email, phone number and password.
  • Provider information: license number, credentials, references and practice details.
  • Usage data: standard analytics and security logs (IP, device, pages viewed).

How we use it

  • To match families with providers and verify insurance benefits.
  • To credential and continuously monitor providers in our network.
  • To communicate appointment, billing and product updates.
  • To improve the platform and prevent fraud or abuse.

Health information

Where we handle Protected Health Information (PHI), we do so under HIPAA-compliant business associate agreements with our subprocessors. Screener results entered on our website live only in your browser unless you choose to share them with a provider.

Sharing

We share information only with: providers you choose to connect with, payment processors, insurance verification services, and law enforcement when legally required. We never sell your information.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing privacy@youraldea.com.

Children

Aldea Connect serves families on behalf of minor children. Accounts are held by a parent or legal guardian. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children under 13.

Contact

Questions? Email privacy@youraldea.com or write to us at 100 E Pine Street, Suite 116, Orlando, FL 32801.
