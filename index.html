Frequently asked

What is Aldea?
The Aldea Directory is a trusted online platform that helps parents find qualified pediatric providers — including speech therapists, psychologists, ABA therapists, occupational therapists and developmental specialists — all in one place. Our mission is to make it easier for families to access care for autism, ADHD, speech delays and early childhood development.
Are the providers on Aldea verified and licensed?
Yes. Every provider holds an active, unrestricted Florida license verified through the state DOH, passes a Level 2 background check, and is manually credentialed by our clinical team before they ever appear in your matches.
Does Aldea accept insurance or private pay?
Both. Most providers are in-network with Aetna, BCBS FL, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, Humana and Tricare. Private pay, HSA and FSA are also accepted. We verify benefits before your first visit.
How does Aldea help me find the right provider?
Tell us about your child in a quick three-minute intake. A care coordinator hand-picks two or three providers who fit your child’s age, location, language and the kind of care you’re looking for — and sends them to you within one business day.
What ages of children does Aldea support?
Ages 0–18. We have providers focused on early intervention, school-age evaluation and adolescent therapy.
How do I schedule an appointment through Aldea?
Once you’re matched, you can book directly from your inbox. Your care coordinator handles benefits verification so there are no surprises at your first visit.
