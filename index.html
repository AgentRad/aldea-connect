Frequently asked

Does Aldea Connect take insurance?

Most providers are in-network with Aetna, BCBS FL, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, Humana and Tricare. We verify benefits before your first visit. Private pay, HSA and FSA are also accepted.

How fast can we actually be seen?

Median match time is 48 hours, median first-visit booking is 7 days. We only list providers with real openings — there is no waitlist on Aldea Connect.

Is this telehealth or in person?

Both. Evaluations and speech are often available via telehealth statewide; ABA and OT are typically in-home or in-clinic in Orlando, Tampa, Miami and Jacksonville.

Who is Aldea Connect for?

Florida families with children ages 0–18 navigating autism, ADHD, learning differences, speech delays, sensory needs or a recent diagnosis — and the clinicians who care for them.

What does it cost to use the platform?

Nothing for families. Providers pay a flat monthly subscription so we never take a cut of your care.

Are you a clinician?

Join a curated network of pediatric and neurodivergent specialists across Florida. Flat monthly subscription, no per-visit fees.
